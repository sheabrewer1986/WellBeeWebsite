# **Version 1.0**
This is it!!! Version 1.0 is official.

With WellBee you now have full control over the health and wellness of the whole family including but not limited to: you, your significant other, your parents, children (young and old) and yes, even your pets.

Organise and monitor all aspects of their health including:

- Appointments: Keep a full history of all vists (doctor, GP, dental, veterinary or otherwise) and track what occured during the visit with tags for scans performed, medication prescribed, and samples taken.
- Diagnoses: Record any diagnosis you receive from the doctor to keep track of all ongoing issues that are affecting the family.
