We regularly improve performance, polish existing features, and introduce new tools based on real user needs. Check back anytime to stay up to date with the latest improvements.

### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**
Version 1.0 is here.

WellBee helps you organise and track the health of your entire family — all in one place. Create individual profiles and keep a clear, structured history of appointments, illnesses, treatments, and everyday health observations.

With WellBee you can manage:

• Appointments — Keep a complete history of medical, dental, or veterinary visits, including scans, prescriptions, and notes.  
• Diagnoses — Track ongoing or past conditions across family members.  
• Growth Metrics — Monitor height, weight, and head circumference trends over time.  
• Injuries — Log bumps, cuts, and incidents alongside treatments given at home.  
• Medications — Record when medication was taken and the exact dosage administered.  
• Observations — Capture behaviours, concerns, or changes you want to monitor.  
• Symptoms — Track symptoms and severity to understand patterns over time.  
• Temperature Logs — Monitor temperature alongside mood and energy levels.

Presented in a clean diary view with light and dark mode support, WellBee gives you a complete health history whenever you need it — especially when speaking with a healthcare professional.
